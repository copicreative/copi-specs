Homepage Rewrite

Prepared for Mortgage Core, LLC · Tim Arrington
A note for Tim

Thanks for taking my call, even quick. I went ahead and put this together so you'd have something real to look at instead of just my pitch.

The core problem: right now your homepage answers "what is a mortgage broker" instead of "why this one." Every section is a generic benefit, secure a loan, lock in terms, shop around. None of it is yours. A visitor can't tell Mortgage Core apart from the ten other tabs they have open. The fix isn't more features, it's a reason to choose you, said in your voice, with your proof visible. And your testimonials, your strongest proof, are tucked behind a separate tab most visitors never click.

Here's what I mean. Your homepage is clean, but it reads like almost every other broker site, "get a great mortgage rate," "we make it easier than ever," "your time and money matter." Those are fine words, but they describe what every broker says they do, so a borrower lands on your page and gets no reason to pick Core over the bank or the broker in the next search result.

What the rewrite does

It gives the page an identity and pulls your proof up front where it can actually work. Every section below keeps the information you have but says it in a way that gives a borrower a reason to choose you, with a note on why each change lifts conversion.

Headline
Current
Get a Great Mortgage Rate. Use one of our quick & easy tools to find out what you qualify for, FREE!
Rewritten
A Mortgage Broker Who Picks Up, Shops It for You, and Closes in About 30 Days.
Your current headline could sit on any broker's site. The new one makes three concrete promises a borrower actually cares about: you're reachable, you do the shopping, and you're fast. Specific promises convert better than "get a great rate," which says nothing a bank doesn't also claim.
"What We Do" Section
Current
We're making getting a mortgage easier than ever before. Mortgage Core, LLC. has an experienced team of brokers on staff.
Rewritten
Most people dread the mortgage process because they're left guessing. We don't work that way. You get one point of contact, straight answers, and a clear picture of what you qualify for before you commit to anything. No runaround, no call center.
"Experienced team on staff" is a claim. Naming the borrower's real fear, being left guessing, and promising the opposite gives them an emotional reason to trust you. People buy from the broker who gets what they're worried about.
Testimonials (move to homepage)
Current
Hidden on a separate Testimonials tab, invisible to most visitors.
Rewritten
Pull two or three of your strongest client reviews directly onto the homepage, right under the headline, with the client's name attached.
Proof a visitor never sees does nothing. Most people don't click to a testimonials tab, they decide on the first screen. Moving real client words onto the homepage is one of the single highest-impact changes you can make, because strangers trust other borrowers more than they trust your copy.
Closing Call to Action
Current
Get a Rate Quote in Just 30 Seconds!
Rewritten
Get a real quote from a real broker, usually same day. Tell us your situation and we'll tell you straight what's possible.
"30 second quote" sounds automated, like every rate-table site. Promising a real person and a straight answer differentiates you from the faceless tools borrowers are tired of.
