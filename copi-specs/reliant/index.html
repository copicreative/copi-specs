Homepage Rewrite

Prepared for Reliant Mortgage Solutions · Sean Trombetti
A note on what you said

When we spoke, you mentioned most of your business comes from referrals, not your website. That's true for a lot of strong brokers, and it's a fair point. But here's the thing we talked about: you rank high on Google, which is exactly how I found you. So strangers with no referral are landing on your site every day, and they have nothing to judge you on but your copy. And even your referral clients Google you before they call. Your site is the trust check that decides whether that warm intro turns into a booked loan.

So your website isn't generating most of your business, but it's quietly deciding how much of it you keep. That's why this rewrite matters. It makes sure that the moment someone lands on your page, referral or stranger, they see exactly why you're the broker to call.

The core problem

Your homepage opens with "Reliable, Responsive, and Knowledgeable." Those are three adjectives every broker claims, so a visitor's eye slides right past them. Meanwhile, the single most powerful fact about your business is buried in your footer hours: you're open seven days a week, including Sunday. No bank does that. Most brokers don't either. That's not a detail, that's a reason to choose you, and right now it's invisible. This rewrite pulls your real edge to the front and turns a list of features into reasons to call.

Headline
Current
Reliable, Responsive, and Knowledgeable.
Rewritten
Your Mortgage Questions Answered Seven Days a Week, Even Sundays.
Your current headline tells people you're responsive. The new one proves it with a fact no competitor can match. A borrower stressed about a deadline reads this and thinks "this is the person who'll actually pick up." It turns a claim into evidence.
Subhead
Current
None. The page jumps straight into loan bullet points.
Rewritten
Whether you found us through a friend or found us on Google, you've reached a broker who answers when it matters, shops 50-plus lenders for your rate, and treats your loan like it's the only one that counts.
This speaks to both your referral clients and the cold traffic your SEO brings in, the strangers who land here with no introduction. It names your responsiveness, your lender access, and your personal attention in one line, before they scroll.
Purchase Section
Current
PURCHASE. As low as 3% down payment on conventional programs and 3.5% on FHA. Use gift funds for the down payment. Use retirement funds for down payment… (feature list)
Rewritten
Buying a home? Most lenders hand you a rate and a checklist. We start with a conversation, because the right loan depends on your situation, not a template. Low down payment options from 3%, zero down for veterans, gift and retirement funds welcome, and real strategy if your credit or debt-to-income needs work. You'll understand every number before you sign anything.
Your current bullets are accurate but cold. This keeps every fact but frames them around the borrower's experience, and it leans on your consultative, responsive style, which is your actual differentiator.
Refinance Section
Current
REFINANCE. No waiting time after you buy a home. Improve your interest rate, refinance with us… (feature list)
Rewritten
Thinking about refinancing? Whether you want a lower payment, cash out for higher-rate debt, a shorter term, or to take someone off the loan, we'll run the real numbers and tell you honestly if it's worth it. No-closing-cost options available. And if the timing isn't right, we'll tell you that too.
The "we'll tell you honestly if it's worth it" line builds trust fast, especially with cold SEO visitors who don't know you yet. It positions you as an advisor, not a salesperson.
Pre-Approval Section
Current
Reliant can give you pre-approval quickly… Sellers will pay attention when you are PRE-APPROVED, not just pre-qualified!
Rewritten
Get truly pre-approved, not just pre-qualified. Sellers take pre-approved buyers seriously, and in a competitive market that's the difference between winning the house and losing it. We run your file through full underwriting up front so your offer carries real weight, and we'll have your detailed quote ready, every number explained.
Your current version has the right idea. This sharpens the stakes, winning or losing the house, which is what the borrower actually fears, and ties it to your speed.
Closing Call to Action
Current
Reliant Mortgage Solutions is proud to offer competitive rates and pricing.
Rewritten
Call or message any day of the week, we mean it. Most lenders make you wait until Monday. We're here when your questions are, because that's when they matter most.
Ends the page by hammering your one unbeatable edge one more time. The last thing they read is the reason you're different.
